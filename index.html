Did you know?

  • Around the world, millions of cats and dogs live as strays, with approximately 200 million stray dogs alone.
  • Stray animals are often victims of neglect or abandonment, but studies show that most can adapt well to family life when adopted.
  • Shelters around the world often microchip and vaccinate adopted animals, providing adopters with healthy pets ready to join a new home.
  • The adoption process also plays a role in reducing the spread of diseases, as shelter animals receive healthcare and spay/neuter services.
  • The Philippine Animal Welfare Society (PAWS) advocates for "Adopt, Don’t Shop," promoting shelter adoption over buying pets.
